Destroy the economy to save it?

Stop inflation in its tracks in this fast-paced arcade shooter.

Inflation is out of control! After years of quantitative easing, the Federal Reserve must reverse course. Who would have guessed that pumping free money into the economy would have negative consequences?

But how do we fight inflation? Address rampant wealth inequality? Fix the exploding costs of healthcare? Build more housing to drive down prices? Bother to address any of the underlying problems with the economy? Nope. Let's raise interest rates!

You are the head of the Federal Reserve, and you have one tool in your central planning toolbox for manipulating the economy: interest rate hikes. This raises the costs of loans between banks, which raises the cost for businesses to borrow money. Those increased costs will lead to layoffs. We can't have any reduction in profits, now can we?

You will know your plan is working by how many jobs you destroy. If people lose their jobs, they won't have money to spend. And if they can't spend money, prices go down and so does inflation. Simple logic.
